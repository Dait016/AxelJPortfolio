About Me

Profile

22 years old, based in Belgium, at the beginning of a modeling journey shaped by Japanese and Korean fashion.

I’m currently at the beginning of my modeling journey, focused on gaining experience, developing my style, and building a strong portfolio.

Japanese and Korean fashion has always interested me. What draws me to it is the versatility and attention to detail, the way an outfit can feel effortless while still making a statement.

Whether it's streetwear, casual looks, or more formal styling, I appreciate how fashion can express personality and confidence.

As I continue to develop as a model, I'm interested in exploring the connection between fashion, culture, and individual style, while building experience across different concepts and creative directions.
